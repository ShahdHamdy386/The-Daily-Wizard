Harry potter movies

From the Sorcerer’s Stone to the Deathly Hallows

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone (Philosopher’s Stone in the UK)

Release Year: 2001

Description: On his eleventh birthday, Harry Potter discovers he’s a wizard and begins his magical journey at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Alongside friends Ron and Hermione, he unravels the mystery of the Sorcerer’s Stone and faces the dark wizard Voldemort for the first time.

Famous quote: It does not do to dwell on dreams and forget to live, remember that.— Albus Dumbledore

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Release Year: 2002

Description: In his second year at Hogwarts, Harry finds himself caught in a mystery as the legendary Chamber of Secrets is opened, unleashing a monster that petrifies students. With Ron and Hermione by his side, Harry must uncover the truth about the heir of Slytherin and save the school from disaster.

Famous quote: It is not our abilities that show what we truly are… it is our choices— Albus Dumbledore

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

Release Year: 2004

Description: In his third year, Harry learns that the dangerous Sirius Black has escaped from Azkaban and is believed to be after him. As Dementors guard the school and secrets from the past are revealed, Harry discovers the truth about his parents, betrayal, and the power of friendship.

Famous quote: Happiness can be found, even in the darkest of times, if one only remembers to turn on the light.— Albus Dumbledore

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

Release Year: 2005

Description: Harry is mysteriously entered into the dangerous Triwizard Tournament, where he must face dragons, mermaids, and a deadly maze. Along the way, he uncovers the terrifying return of Lord Voldemort, marking the true beginning of the Second Wizarding War.

Famous quote: If you want to know what a man’s like, take a good look at how he treats his inferiors, not his equals.— Sirius Black

Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

Release Year: 2007

Description: As the Ministry of Magic refuses to acknowledge Voldemort’s return, Harry and his friends form Dumbledore’s Army to fight back. Harry struggles with visions connected to Voldemort, while Dolores Umbridge’s oppressive rule at Hogwarts sparks rebellion.

Famous quote: You’re not a bad person. You’re a very good person, who bad things have happened to. You’ve got a choice about what you’re going to be.— Sirius Black

Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

Release Year: 2009

Description: Dark times fall on the wizarding world as Voldemort’s power grows. At Hogwarts, Harry discovers an old Potions book belonging to the mysterious Half-Blood Prince. With Dumbledore’s guidance, he uncovers Voldemort’s past and the secret of the Horcruxes.

Famous quote: It is the unknown we fear when we look upon death and darkness, nothing more.— Albus Dumbledore

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1

Release Year: 2010

Description: Harry, Ron, and Hermione leave Hogwarts behind to destroy Voldemort’s Horcruxes. On the run and hunted by Death Eaters, they uncover the legend of the Deathly Hallows while struggling with fear, loss, and mistrust.

Famous quote: The last enemy that shall be destroyed is death.— Inscription on Harry’s parents’ gravestone

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2

Release Year: 2011

Description: The final battle for Hogwarts begins. Harry faces Voldemort in the ultimate showdown as friends, teachers, and students unite to defend the castle. Sacrifice, bravery, and love define the end of the saga.

Famous quote: Do not pity the dead, Harry. Pity the living, and above all, those who live without love.— Albus Dumbledore
